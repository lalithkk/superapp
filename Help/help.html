Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on ‘Forgot Password’ at login. You'll receive a reset link via email or OTP on mobile.

How can I cancel my order?

Go to ‘My Orders’, choose the order and click on ‘Cancel’. Cancellation is subject to policy.

When will I get my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 working days post cancellation and sent to original payment mode.
